Batman arkham vr

Batman: Arkham VR is a 2016 virtual reality action-adventure video game developed by Rocksteady Studios and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment for the PlayStation 4 and Microsoft Windows. Based on the DC Comics superhero Batman, it is a part of the Batman: Arkham series and the first installment to use virtual reality headsets, allowing the player to experience the game world from Batman's perspective.

Arkham VR was released worldwide on October 11, 2016 for the PlayStation 4 and on April 25, 2017 for Microsoft Windows.
